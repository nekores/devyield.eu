About Us

DevYield, in short “DY”

DY is an emerging European enterprise established by a team of social entrepreneurs having experience in international development and background in sustainable farming practices. DY went international with its first company office in Lisbon, Portugal. DY is legally incorporated in Portugal as "The Sociedade por quotas (LDA).

Sectors

What We Do?

Nonprofit Consulting Services

Our expertise lies in aiding the nonprofit sector by employing specialized approaches rooted in Result Based Management (RBM). Our services encompass the entire spectrum of design, planning, monitoring, evaluation, and research. We are dedicated to enhancing the outcomes of humanitarian agencies, international development organizations, and projects funded by donors within developing countries.

Digital Technology Products and Services

We assist businesses in leveraging tech innovation, digitization, and emerging tech to enhance processes, tackle challenges, and boost productivity. By strategically integrating new technologies, we help companies streamline operations, elevate customer experiences, and stay competitive. Our mission is to empower businesses to innovate and thrive through technology, driving efficiency and growth. Our goal is to catalyze a future where technology is not just a tool, but a crucial driver of success.

Agribusiness Production and related services

Driven by a deep commitment to nature and sustainability, we're dedicated to crafting varied agricultural products through eco-friendly farming methods. Our commitment extends to delivering agribusiness services to local farmers and agribusinesses, promoting sustainable practices across rural Portugal. Our goal is to generate green employment and contribute to global challenges like climate change, food insecurity, and unemployment specifically migrant workers.

HR Sourcing and Talent Pool Management

Specializing in HR services for IT, Retail, and Aagriculture Sector, we aim to bridge the gap between businesses and job seekers. Our mission is twofold: assisting businesses in securing skilled and talented workforce and empowering employees to discover fulfilling employment opportunities that unlock their full potential. Through our expertise and networks, we strive to foster a mutually beneficial environment where both businesses and workers thrive.

Tourism and Hospitality Services

We are embarking on a journey to introduce exceptional lodging, Cafe, and travel experiences, we're dedicated to transforming travels and vocations into cherished memories. Our focus extends beyond conventional tourism, as we're committed to promoting eco-friendly practices in everything we do. Our goals is to promote sustainable adventures that not only enrich the human soul but also contribute to the preservation of our planet. Stay tuned for the launch of our comprehensive Tourism and Hospitality Services.

Real Estate and Construction

Exploring the realm of Real Estate and Construction, our vision encompasses investments in eco-conscious properties for both residential and commercial domains. Our mission revolves around crafting structures that embrace net-zero carbon methodologies, prioritizing well-being, and pioneering climate-resilient edifices. Stay tuned as we endeavor to redefine the future of construction by harmonizing sustainability, comfort, and resilience in every building we create.
